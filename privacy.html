This Privacy Policy describes how Metric Networks ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when visiting www.metricnetworks.net or interacting with us through our website — including our contact form and AI-assisted chat widget. We are committed to your privacy and handle your information with care.

01

Who We Are

Metric Networks is an IT solutions and enterprise network consulting firm headquartered in Knoxville, Tennessee, USA. We specialize in data backup, business continuity planning, enterprise network design and deployment, and virtualization migrations (including VMware to Proxmox). Our website serves as an informational resource and a way for prospective clients to reach our team.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide our services. Information is gathered in three ways:

A. Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following fields:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Client type (e.g., small business, enterprise, individual)
  • Service interest (e.g., data backup, network consulting, Proxmox migration)
  • Message — the content of your inquiry

Providing this information is entirely voluntary. You are not required to submit the form to access our website.

B. Chat Widget (Metric Assistant) Chatbot

Our website includes an interactive chat assistant ("Metric") powered by a decision-tree chatbot. If you choose to engage with the chat widget and request a consultation or callback, the bot will ask you to provide:

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Phone number
  • Service interest (selected from a menu of options such as Networking, Managed IT, Backup & Data Protection, or General Inquiry)

This information is collected only if you actively choose to request a consultation through the chat flow. Simply viewing or navigating the chat widget without submitting your contact details does not result in data collection. Once you confirm submission, your details are sent to our team via Web3Forms (see Section 4).

The chat widget does not use artificial intelligence that processes free-text input in a way that stores conversational data. It is a button-driven decision-tree with optional free-text fields limited to name, company, and phone number. No chat transcripts are retained beyond the lead submission.

C. Information Collected Automatically

Our website uses the following technologies that collect data automatically:

  • Cloudflare Pages (CDN & Security) — Our hosting provider automatically collects technical data including your IP address (for DDoS mitigation and bot detection), browser type and version, referring URL and requested page, and date/time of access. This is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. GA4 collects anonymized data including pages visited, time on page, referral source, general geographic region (country/city level), device type, and browser. GA4 does not collect your name, email, or phone number. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can opt out of GA4 tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics — We also use Cloudflare's privacy-focused analytics beacon, which collects aggregate page view data without using cookies or fingerprinting. No personal information is collected by this service.
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide through our contact form or chat widget for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry — We reply to your question, request, or service interest via email or phone.
  • To route your inquiry — We direct your request to the appropriate member of our team based on your stated service interest.
  • To follow up on a consultation request — If you book a consultation through the chat widget, we use your name, company, phone number, and service interest to schedule and conduct that follow-up.
  • To improve our website — Anonymized analytics data (GA4 and Cloudflare Analytics) is used to understand which pages are most useful and to improve site performance and content.

We do not use your information for mass marketing campaigns, sell it to lead brokers, or combine it with third-party data profiles. We will only contact you in direct response to your submitted inquiry or consultation request unless you explicitly request otherwise.

04

How We Share Your Information

We share your information only to the limited extent necessary to operate our website and deliver your inquiry to our inbox. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

Service Providers We Use:

  • Web3Forms — Both our contact form and our chat widget use the Web3Forms API to deliver submitted data to our email inbox. Web3Forms receives the fields you submit (name, email, phone, company, service interest, and/or message) solely to transmit them to us. Web3Forms acts as a data processor on our behalf. Please review Web3Forms' Privacy Policy for details.
  • Cloudflare Pages & Web Analytics — Our website is hosted on Cloudflare's global CDN. All web traffic passes through Cloudflare's network, which provides performance, security, and privacy-focused analytics services. Please review Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Analytics 4 — Anonymized, aggregated website usage data is shared with Google for analytics purposes. Google may process this data on servers located outside your country of residence. Google does not receive your name, email, phone number, or any information you submit via the contact form or chat widget. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Beyond these infrastructure and analytics providers, we do not share your personal information with any other third parties unless required to do so by applicable law or a valid legal process (such as a court order or subpoena).

05

Data Retention

We retain inquiry emails — including the personal information contained in them — for as long as reasonably necessary to service your request and maintain business records in accordance with standard accounting and legal practices (typically up to three years for business correspondence).

Chat widget lead submissions are delivered to our email inbox via Web3Forms and are retained in that inbox under the same policy as contact form submissions. No chat transcript or lead data is stored in a separate database on our website infrastructure.

If you would like your information deleted, you may submit a deletion request to support@metricnetworks.net. We will remove your personal data from our records within 30 days of receiving a verified request, except where we are required by law to retain it or where it is necessary to complete an ongoing service engagement you initiated.

Cloudflare, Web3Forms, and Google each maintain their own retention policies for infrastructure, transactional, and analytics logs; please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

06

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption — All data in transit between your browser and our website is encrypted via TLS/SSL.
  • Cloudflare WAF — Our site is protected by Cloudflare's Web Application Firewall, which filters malicious traffic and bot requests before they reach our infrastructure.
  • Encrypted API keys — Our Web3Forms integration key is embedded in our website for form delivery. While the key is technically accessible in client-side code (as required by the Web3Forms service), its scope is limited to form submission and cannot be used to read, access, or export submitted data.
  • No server-side database — We do not operate a database of user records on our web infrastructure. Form and chat submissions are delivered directly to our email inbox and are not stored in a web-accessible location.
  • Minimal data collection — The chat widget is a button-driven decision tree. It does not process or retain free-text conversation history beyond the specific fields (name, company, phone) you choose to submit.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have reason to believe your interaction with us has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access — You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request that we provide your information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to processing of your personal information where we rely on legitimate interest as our legal basis.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@metricnetworks.net. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

International Users / GDPR

Our services are primarily directed at businesses and individuals in the United States. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent legislation.

Legal Bases for Processing:

  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — When you submit a contact form or chat widget lead, we process your information to respond to your business inquiry, which constitutes a legitimate interest. We have determined that this interest is not overridden by your privacy rights given the limited scope of data collected and the direct, expected nature of the contact.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — By voluntarily submitting the contact form or confirming your details through the chat widget, you provide consent for us to contact you in response to your inquiry.
  • Legitimate Interest — Analytics — We use Google Analytics 4 to analyze anonymized website usage data to improve our services. We rely on legitimate interest for this processing given the pseudonymized and aggregated nature of the data.

As our business and infrastructure are based in the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. We rely on standard contractual clauses and processor agreements with Cloudflare, Web3Forms, and Google to safeguard such transfers.

If you have concerns about our data practices or wish to lodge a complaint, you have the right to contact your local data protection supervisory authority.

Cookies & Analytics

We use a minimal cookie footprint. We do not use advertising cookies or any tracking technologies for behavioral profiling or retargeting.

The following cookies and analytics technologies are active on this website:

  • __cf_bm (Cloudflare Bot Management) — Set automatically by Cloudflare to distinguish human visitors from automated bots. This is a security-essential cookie that expires after 30 minutes of inactivity. It does not track you across websites and contains no personal information. It is strictly necessary for the secure operation of our website.
  • Google Analytics 4 cookies (_ga, _ga_*) — Set by GA4 to distinguish unique visitors and sessions for analytics purposes. These cookies persist for up to 2 years. They collect anonymized browsing data and do not collect your name, email, or phone number. You may opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics beacon — A lightweight, cookieless analytics script that collects aggregate page view data. It does not set cookies, does not fingerprint your device, and collects no personal information.

The GA4 cookies are non-essential. By continuing to use our website, you acknowledge their presence. If you prefer not to be tracked, you may use the GA opt-out tool linked above or enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings, though we cannot guarantee all third-party services will honor that signal.
