This Privacy Policy describes how Metric Networks ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when visiting www.metricnetworks.net or contacting us through our website. We are committed to your privacy and handle your information with care.

01

Who We Are

Metric Networks is an IT solutions and enterprise network consulting firm headquartered in Knoxville, Tennessee, USA. We specialize in data backup, business continuity planning, enterprise network design and deployment, and virtualization migrations (including VMware to Proxmox). Our website serves as an informational resource and a way for prospective clients to reach our team.

Data Controller: Metric Networks · Knoxville, Tennessee, USA
Email: support@metricnetworks.net · Phone: 865.269.2907
02

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide our services. Information is gathered in two ways:

A. Information You Provide Directly

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following fields:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Client type (e.g., small business, enterprise, individual)
  • Service interest (e.g., data backup, network consulting, Proxmox migration)
  • Message — the content of your inquiry

Providing this information is entirely voluntary. You are not required to submit the form to access our website.

B. Information Collected Automatically

Our hosting provider, Cloudflare Pages, automatically collects certain technical data as part of standard CDN and security operations. This may include:

  • Your IP address (used for DDoS mitigation and bot detection)
  • Browser type and version
  • Referring URL and requested page
  • Date and time of access

We do not install any analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics) and we do not use tracking pixels or behavioral advertising cookies. Cloudflare's data practices are governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit through our contact form for one primary purpose: to respond to your inquiry. Specifically, we may use it to:

  • Reply to your question, request, or service interest via email or phone
  • Route your inquiry to the appropriate member of our team
  • Follow up on an ongoing engagement or sales conversation at your request

We do not use your information for mass marketing campaigns, sell it to lead brokers, or combine it with third-party data profiles. We will only contact you in direct response to your submitted inquiry unless you explicitly request otherwise.

04

How We Share Your Information

We share your information only to the limited extent necessary to deliver it to our inbox. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

Service Providers We Use:

  • Web3Forms — Our contact form uses the Web3Forms API to deliver your submitted form data to our email inbox. Web3Forms receives the form fields you submit (name, email, phone, client type, service interest, and message) solely to transmit them to us. Web3Forms acts as a data processor on our behalf. Please review Web3Forms' Privacy Policy for details.
  • Cloudflare Pages — Our website is hosted on Cloudflare's global CDN. All web traffic passes through Cloudflare's network, which provides performance and security services including DDoS protection, a Web Application Firewall (WAF), and bot management. Cloudflare may process technical metadata (IP addresses, request headers) as part of this infrastructure. Please review Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

Beyond these two infrastructure providers, we do not share your personal information with any other third parties unless required to do so by applicable law or a valid legal process (such as a court order or subpoena).

05

Data Retention

We retain inquiry emails — including the personal information contained in them — for as long as reasonably necessary to service your request and maintain business records in accordance with standard accounting and legal practices (typically up to three years for business correspondence).

If you would like your information deleted, you may submit a deletion request to support@metricnetworks.net. We will remove your personal data from our records within 30 days of receiving a verified request, except where we are required by law to retain it or where it is necessary to complete an ongoing service engagement you initiated.

Cloudflare and Web3Forms maintain their own retention policies for infrastructure and transactional logs; please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

06

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption — All data in transit between your browser and our website is encrypted via TLS/SSL.
  • Cloudflare WAF — Our site is protected by Cloudflare's Web Application Firewall, which filters malicious traffic and bot requests before they reach our infrastructure.
  • Encrypted API keys — Our Web3Forms integration key is stored as an environment variable and never exposed in client-side source code in a manner that would allow unauthorized form submissions on your behalf.
  • No server-side database — We do not operate a database of user records; form submissions are delivered directly to our email inbox and are not stored in a web-accessible location.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have reason to believe your interaction with us has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access — You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request that we provide your information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to processing of your personal information where we rely on legitimate interest as our legal basis.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@metricnetworks.net. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

08

California Privacy Rights / CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) may grant you additional rights.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising. Because we do not sell or share data in these ways, we do not offer a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" opt-out link — there is nothing to opt out of.

California residents may still exercise the rights described in Section 7 (access, deletion, correction, and portability) by contacting us at support@metricnetworks.net. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

Categories of personal information collected in the past 12 months: identifiers (name, email, phone), commercial information (service interests), and internet activity (Cloudflare technical logs). All collected solely for responding to inquiries.

09

International Users / GDPR

Our services are primarily directed at businesses and individuals in the United States. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent legislation.

Legal Bases for Processing:

  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — When you submit a contact form, we process your information to respond to your business inquiry, which constitutes a legitimate interest. We have determined that this interest is not overridden by your privacy rights given the limited scope of data collected and the direct, expected nature of the contact.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — By voluntarily submitting the contact form, you provide consent for us to contact you in response to your inquiry.

As our business and infrastructure are based in the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. We rely on standard contractual clauses and processor agreements with Cloudflare and Web3Forms to safeguard such transfers.

If you have concerns about our data practices or wish to lodge a complaint, you have the right to contact your local data protection supervisory authority.

10

Cookies

We use a minimal cookie footprint. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any tracking technologies for behavioral profiling.

The only cookie set on this website is:

  • __cf_bm (Cloudflare Bot Management) — This cookie is set automatically by Cloudflare to distinguish human visitors from automated bots. It is a security-essential cookie that expires after 30 minutes of inactivity. It does not track you across websites and contains no personal information about you as an individual. This cookie is strictly necessary for the secure operation of our website.

Because we set no non-essential cookies, no cookie consent banner is required. If you have concerns about this cookie, please review Cloudflare's Cookie Policy.

Children's Privacy

If you believe that a child under 13 has submitted personal information through our contact form, please contact us immediately at support@metricnetworks.net and we will delete that information promptly.
